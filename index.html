It was a dark and stormy night...

The lights flickered as the rain beat down.

Abigail paced the drawing room, waiting for Hobbes to arrive.

A loud knock from the door echoed in the room.

 

The body lay in front of the fireplace, next to the wrought iron ash shovel, which was stained with blood.

Abigail shrieked, covering her mouth with a hand as she backed away. Hobbes was nowhere to be seen.

What should she do?
